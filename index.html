WhyAssessment Project

Welcome to WhyAssessment.github.io, a platform dedicated to helping adolescents discover their "Why" in life based on Simon Sinek's insights into the power of purpose.

Project Overview

This platform serves as an assessment tool designed to guide adolescents in identifying their intrinsic motivations and values. The ultimate goal is to empower them to make informed decisions aligned with their sense of purpose.
